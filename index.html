About

Wohlstand Security, located in Roatan, Honduras, is dedicated to providing a wide array of security services tailored to meet your needs. From VIP protection to site security and expert consulting, we offer comprehensive solutions for risk assessment, transportation, medical services, and seamless coordination with authorities. Our primary objective is to establish safe environments while encouraging community engagement.
